Go Clean, Go Green

Home News Join out mission

A project inspired by former internationally acclaimed magician turned green activist in his community, Prince Rashid Juma. The Go Green Go Clean project in Eldorado Park Extension 8 was initiated over 10 years ago. Together with some volunteers, Rashid decided to turn their trash into treasure.

Missions:

  • Create and maintain a healthy, green, and clean environment
  • Empower and educate the community and youth through skills development about a clean and green environment to Re-use, Re-duce, and Re-cycle.
  • To start a micro-business flea market and create job opportunities
  • Uplift the community of Eldorado Park and stimulate our economy.
